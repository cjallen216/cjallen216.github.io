About Me

Hi, my name is Chris Allen... no, I'm not the Season 8 winner of American Idol, but I have met him!

I've always loved LEGO and puzzles because I enjoy the process of how everything comes together to create something special.

Before getting into coding, I was a webmaster and digital marketer for almost 20 years, mainly in radio. Once I reached the pinnacle of that career path, I realized in order to make myself more competitive in the industry, I need to learn programming.

I can offer both creative and strategic approaches to solving problems. My goal is to be not only personally fulfilled but also have a positive impact on the world.

While I may not sing like an Idol winner, I can definitely help your code perform like one!
